“For insight into greed, four things should be developed.
What four?
Firstly, a [[mendicant]] meditates by observing an aspect of the [[body]]—keen, aware, and mindful, rid of [[covetous]]ness and displeasure for the world.
They meditate observing an aspect of feelings … mind … principles—keen, aware, and mindful, rid of [[covetous]]ness and displeasure for the world.
For insight into greed, these four things should be developed.”
