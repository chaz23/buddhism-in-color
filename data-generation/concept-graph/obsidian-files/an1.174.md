“One person, [[mendicant]]s, arises in the world unique, without peer or counterpart, incomparable, matchless, unrivaled, unequaled, without equal, the best of men.
What one person?
The Realized One, the [[perfected one]], the fully awakened Buddha.
This is the one person, [[mendicant]]s, who arises in the world unique, without peer or counterpart, incomparable, matchless, unrivaled, unequaled, without equal, the best of men.”
