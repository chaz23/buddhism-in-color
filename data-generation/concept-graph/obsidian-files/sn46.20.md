“Mendicants, the seven awakening factors, when developed and cultivated, lead solely to [[disillusion]]ment, [[dispassion]], [[cessation]], peace, insight, awakening, and extinguishment.
What seven?
The awakening factors of mindfulness, investigation of principles, energy, rapture, tranquility, [[immersion]], and equanimity.
These seven awakening factors, when developed and cultivated, lead solely to [[disillusion]]ment, [[dispassion]], [[cessation]], peace, insight, awakening, and extinguishment.”
