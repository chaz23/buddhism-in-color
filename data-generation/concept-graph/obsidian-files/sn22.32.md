At Sāvatthī.
“Mendicants, I will teach you the breakable and the unbreakable.
Listen …
And what is the breakable? What is the unbreakable?
Form is breakable,
but its [[cessation]], settling, and ending is unbreakable.
Feeling …
perception …
choices …
[[consciousness]] is breakable,
but its [[cessation]], settling, and ending is unbreakable.”
