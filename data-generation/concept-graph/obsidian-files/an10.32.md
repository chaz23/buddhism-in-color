“Sir, how many grounds are there to suspend the recitation of the monastic code?”
“Upāli, there are ten grounds to suspend the recitation of the monastic code.
What ten?
A [[mendicant]] who has committed an expulsion offense is sitting in the [[assembly]]. A discussion about whether someone has committed an expulsion offense is unfinished. A person who is not fully ordained is sitting in the [[assembly]]. A discussion about whether someone is fully ordained or not is unfinished. Someone who has resigned the training is sitting in the [[assembly]]. A discussion about whether someone has rejected the training or not is unfinished. A eunuch is sitting in the [[assembly]]. A discussion about whether someone is a eunuch is unfinished. A raper of [[nun]]s is sitting in the [[assembly]]. A discussion about whether or not someone is a raper of [[nun]]s is unfinished.
These are the ten grounds to suspend the recitation of the monastic code.”
