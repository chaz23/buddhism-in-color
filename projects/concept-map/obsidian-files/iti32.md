This was said by the Buddha, the Perfected One: that is what I heard.
“Mendicants, a person with two qualities is cast down to hell.
What two?
Bad [[conduct]] and bad view.
A person who has these two qualities is cast down to hell.”
That is what the Buddha said.
On this it is said:
“If a person possesses
these two qualities—
bad [[conduct]]
and bad views—
when their [[body]] breaks up, that witless person
is reborn in hell.”
This too was spoken by the Blessed One: that is what I heard.
