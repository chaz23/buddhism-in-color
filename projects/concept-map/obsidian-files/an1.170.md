“One person, [[mendicant]]s, arises in the world for the welfare and happiness of the people, out of [[compassion]] for the world, for the benefit, welfare, and happiness of [[gods]] and humans.
What one person?
The Realized One, the [[perfected one]], the fully awakened Buddha.
This is the one person, [[mendicant]]s, who arises in the world for the welfare and happiness of the people, out of [[compassion]] for the world, for the benefit, welfare, and happiness of [[gods]] and humans.”
