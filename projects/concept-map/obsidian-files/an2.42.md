“There are, [[mendicant]]s, these two [[assembly]].
What two?
A shallow [[assembly]] and a deep [[assembly]].
And what is a shallow [[assembly]]?
An [[assembly]] where the [[mendicant]]s are restless, insolent, fickle, scurrilous, loose-tongued, unmindful, lacking situational awareness and [[immersion]], with straying minds and un[[discipline]] faculties.
This is called a shallow [[assembly]].
And what is a deep [[assembly]]?
An [[assembly]] where the [[mendicant]]s are not restless, insolent, fickle, scurrilous, or loose-tongued, but have established mindfulness, situational awareness, [[immersion]], unified minds, and restrained faculties.
This is called a deep [[assembly]].
These are the two [[assembly]].
The better of these two [[assembly]] is the deep [[assembly]].”
