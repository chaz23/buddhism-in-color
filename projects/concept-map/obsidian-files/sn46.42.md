“Mendicants, when a Wheel-Turning Monarch appears seven [[treasure]]s appear.
What seven?
The wheel, the elephant, the horse, the jewel, the woman, the [[treasure]]r, and the counselor.
When a Wheel-Turning Monarch appears these seven [[treasure]]s appear.
When a Realized One, a [[perfected one]], a fully awakened Buddha appears the seven [[treasure]]s of the awakening factors appear.
What seven?
The [[treasure]]s of the awakening factors of mindfulness, investigation of principles, energy, rapture, tranquility, [[immersion]], and equanimity.
When a Realized One, a [[perfected one]], a fully awakened Buddha appears these seven [[treasure]]s of the awakening factors appear.”
