“Mendicants, there are these six faculties.
What six?
The faculties of the eye, ear, nose, tongue, [[body]], and mind.
As long as I didn’t truly understand these six faculties’ gratification, drawback, and escape for what they are, I didn’t announce my supreme perfect awakening in this world with its [[gods]], Māras, and Brahmās, this population with its ascetics and [[brahmin]]s, its [[gods]] and humans.
But when I did truly understand these six faculties’ gratification, drawback, and escape for what they are, I announced my supreme perfect awakening in this world with its [[gods]], Māras, and Brahmās, this population with its ascetics and [[brahmin]]s, its [[gods]] and humans.
Knowledge and vision arose in me:
‘My freedom is unshakable; this is my last [[rebirth]]; now there’ll be no more future lives.’”
