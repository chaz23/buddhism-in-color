At Sāvatthī.
“Mendicants, there are these four elements.
What four?
The elements of earth, water, fire, and [[air element]].
There are ascetics and [[brahmin]]s who don’t truly understand these four elements’ gratification, drawback, and escape for what they are.
I don’t regard them as true ascetics and [[brahmin]]s. Those venerables don’t realize the goal of life as an ascetic or [[brahmin]], and don’t live having realized it with their own insight.
There are ascetics and [[brahmin]]s who do truly understand these four elements’ gratification, drawback, and escape for what they are.
I regard them as true ascetics and [[brahmin]]s. Those venerables realize the goal of life as an ascetic or [[brahmin]], and live having realized it with their own insight.”
