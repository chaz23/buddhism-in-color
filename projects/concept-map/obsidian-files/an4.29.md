“Mendicants, these four basic principles are primordial, long-standing, traditional, and ancient. They are uncorrupted, as they have been since the beginning. They’re not being corrupted now nor will they be. Sensible ascetics and [[brahmin]]s don’t look down on them.
What four?
Contentment, good will, right mindfulness, and right [[immersion]].
These four basic principles are primordial, long-standing, traditional, and ancient. They are uncorrupted, as they have been since the beginning. They’re not being corrupted now nor will they be. Sensible ascetics and [[brahmin]]s don’t look down on them.
You should live with [[content]]ment,
and a heart of good will,
mindful, with unified mind,
serene within.”
