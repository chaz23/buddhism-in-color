“Mendicants, someone with six qualities is cast down to hell.
What six?
They kill living creatures, steal, commit sexual mis[[conduct]], and lie. And they have wicked [[desire]]s and wrong view.
Someone with these six qualities is cast down to hell.
Someone with six qualities is raised up to heaven.
What six?
They don’t kill living creatures, steal, commit sexual mis[[conduct]], or lie. And they have few [[desire]]s and right view.
Someone with these six qualities is raised up to heaven.”
