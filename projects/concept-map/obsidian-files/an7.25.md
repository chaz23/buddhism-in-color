“Mendicants, I will teach you seven principles that prevent decline.
Listen and pay close attention …
And what are the seven principles that prevent decline?
As long as the [[mendicant]]s are faithful …
conscientious …
prudent …
learned …
energetic …
mindful …
wise, they can expect growth, not decline.
As long as these seven principles that prevent decline last among the [[mendicant]]s, and as long as the [[mendicant]]s are seen following them, they can expect growth, not decline.”
