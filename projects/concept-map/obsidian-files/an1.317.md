“One person, [[mendicant]]s, arises in the world for the welfare and happiness of the people, for the benefit, welfare, and happiness of [[gods]] and humans.
What one person?
Someone with right view, whose perspective is undistorted.
They draw many people away from false teachings and establish them in the true teaching.
This is one person who arises in the world for the welfare and happiness of the people, for the benefit, welfare, and happiness of [[gods]] and humans.”
