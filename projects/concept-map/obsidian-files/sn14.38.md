At Sāvatthī.
“Mendicants, there are these four elements.
What four?
The elements of earth, water, fire, and [[air element]].
There are ascetics and [[brahmin]]s who don’t truly understand these four elements’ origin, ending, gratification, drawback, and escape …
There are ascetics and [[brahmin]]s who do truly understand these four elements’ origin, ending, gratification, drawback, and escape …”
