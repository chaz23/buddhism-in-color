This was said by the Buddha, the Perfected One: that is what I heard.
“Taking into account exterior factors, [[mendicant]]s, I do not see a single one that is so very helpful as good friendship for a [[trainee]] [[mendicant]] who hasn’t achieved their heart’s [[desire]], but lives aspiring to the supreme sanctuary.
A [[mendicant]] who has good friends gives up the unskillful and develops the skillful.”
That is what the Buddha said.
On this it is said:
“A [[mendicant]] with good friends
is reverential and respectful
when their friends are speaking,
aware and mindful.
Gradually they would attain
the ending of all fetters.”
This too was spoken by the Blessed One: that is what I heard.
