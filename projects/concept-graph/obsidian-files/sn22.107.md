At Sāvatthī.
“Mendicants, there are these five grasping [[aggregates]].
What five?
That is, the grasping [[aggregates]] of form, feeling, perception, choices, and [[consciousness]].
There are ascetics and [[brahmin]]s who don’t truly understand these five grasping [[aggregates]]’ gratification, drawback, and escape …
There are ascetics and [[brahmin]]s who do truly understand …”
