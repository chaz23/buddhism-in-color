This was said by the Buddha, the Perfected One: that is what I heard.
“Mendicants, a person with two qualities is raised up to heaven.
What two?
Excellent [[conduct]] and excellent view.
A person who has these two qualities is is raised up to heaven.”
The Buddha spoke this matter.
On this it is said:
“If a person possesses
these two qualities—
excellent [[conduct]]
and excellent views—
when their [[body]] breaks up, that wise person
is reborn in heaven.”
This too is a matter that was spoken by the Blessed One: that is what I heard.
