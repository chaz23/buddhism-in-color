“Mendicants, you shouldn’t associate with a [[mendicant]] friend who has five qualities.
What five?
They start up work projects. They take up [[disciplinary issue]]s. They [[conflict]] with leading [[mendicant]]s. They like long and aimless wandering. They’re unable to educate, encourage, fire up, and inspire you from time to time with a Dhamma talk.
Mendicants, you shouldn’t associate with a [[mendicant]] friend who has these five qualities.
You should associate with a [[mendicant]] friend who has five qualities.
What five?
They don’t start up work projects. They don’t take up [[disciplinary issue]]s. They don’t [[conflict]] with leading [[mendicant]]s. They don’t like long and aimless wandering. They’re able to educate, encourage, fire up, and inspire you from time to time with a Dhamma talk.
You should associate with a [[mendicant]] friend who has these five qualities.”
