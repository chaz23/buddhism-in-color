What six?
It’s when a [[mendicant]] has many [[desire]]s—they’re frustrated and not [[content]] with any kind of robes, [[almsfood]], lodgings, and medicines and supplies for the sick. And they’re faithless, unethical, unmindful, and witless.
A [[mendicant]] with these six qualities can expect decline, not growth, in skillful qualities, whether by day or by night.
