At Sāvatthī.
“Mendicants, [[sentient being]]s come together and converge because of an element.
Those who have bad convictions come together and converge with those who have bad convictions.
Those who have good convictions come together and converge with those who have good convictions.
In the past, too, [[sentient being]]s came together and converged because of an element. …
In the future, too, [[sentient being]]s will come together and converge because of an element. …
At present, too, [[sentient being]]s come together and converge because of an element.
Those who have bad convictions come together and converge with those who have bad convictions.
Those who have good convictions come together and converge with those who have good convictions.”
