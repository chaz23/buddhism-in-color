“Mendicants, these ten things when developed and cultivated don’t lead solely to [[disillusion]]ment, [[dispassion]], [[cessation]], peace, insight, awakening, and extinguishment apart from the Holy One’s training.
What ten?
Right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right [[immersion]], right knowledge, and right freedom.
These are the ten things that when developed and cultivated don’t lead solely to [[disillusion]]ment, [[dispassion]], [[cessation]], peace, insight, awakening, and extinguishment apart from the Holy One’s training.”
