“Mendicants, I will teach you the practice fit for uprooting all [[conceive]]ing.
Listen and apply your mind well, I will speak. …
And what is the practice fit for uprooting all [[conceive]]ing?
It’s when a [[mendicant]] does not [[conceive]]e anything to be the eye, does not [[conceive]]e it in the eye, does not [[conceive]]e it as the eye, and does not [[conceive]]e that ‘the eye is mine.’
They don’t [[conceive]]e anything to be sights, they don’t [[conceive]]e it in sights, they don’t [[conceive]]e it as sights, and they don’t [[conceive]]e that ‘sights are mine.’
They don’t [[conceive]]e eye [[consciousness]] …
eye [[contact]] …
They don’t [[conceive]]e anything to be the pleasant, painful, or neutral feeling that arises conditioned by eye [[contact]]. They don’t [[conceive]]e it in that, they don’t [[conceive]]e it as that, and they don’t [[conceive]]e that ‘that is mine.’
They don’t [[conceive]]e anything to be the ear … nose … tongue …
[[body]] …
mind …
They don’t [[conceive]]e anything to be the pleasant, painful, or neutral feeling that arises conditioned by mind [[contact]]. They don’t [[conceive]]e it in that, they don’t [[conceive]]e it as that, and they don’t [[conceive]]e that ‘that is mine.’
They don’t [[conceive]]e anything to be all, they don’t [[conceive]]e it in all, they don’t [[conceive]]e it as all, and they don’t [[conceive]]e that ‘all is mine.’
Not [[conceive]]ing, they don’t grasp at anything in the world.
Not grasping, they’re not anxious. Not being anxious, they personally become extinguished.
They understand: ‘Rebirth is ended, the spiritual journey has been completed, what had to be done has been done, there is no return to any state of existence.’
This is the practice fit for uprooting all [[conceive]]ing.”
