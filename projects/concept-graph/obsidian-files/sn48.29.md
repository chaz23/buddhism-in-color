“Mendicants, there are these six faculties.
What six?
The faculties of the eye, ear, nose, tongue, [[body]], and mind.
There are ascetics and [[brahmin]]s who don’t truly understand the origin, ending, gratification, drawback, and escape when it comes to these six faculties.
I don’t deem them as true ascetics and [[brahmin]]s. Those venerables don’t realize the goal of life as an ascetic or [[brahmin]], and don’t live having realized it with their own insight.
There are ascetics and [[brahmin]]s who do truly understand the origin, ending, gratification, drawback, and escape when it comes to these six faculties.
I deem them as true ascetics and [[brahmin]]s. Those venerables realize the goal of life as an ascetic or [[brahmin]], and live having realized it with their own insight.”
