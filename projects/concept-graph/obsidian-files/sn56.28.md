“Mendicants, there are these four noble truths.
What four?
The noble truths of suffering, the origin of suffering, the [[cessation]] of suffering, and the practice that leads to the [[cessation]] of suffering.
In this world with its [[gods]], Māras, and Brahmās, this population with its ascetics and [[brahmin]]s, its [[gods]] and humans, the Realized One is the Noble One.
That’s why they’re called ‘noble truths’.
That’s why you should practice meditation …”
