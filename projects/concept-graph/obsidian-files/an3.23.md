“These three people are found in the world.
What three?
Firstly, a certain person makes hurtful choices by way of [[body]], speech, and mind.
Having made these choices, they’re reborn in a hurtful world,
where hurtful [[contact]]s strike them.
Touched by hurtful [[contact]]s, they experience hurtful feelings that are exclusively painful—like the beings in hell.
Furthermore, another person makes pleasing choices by way of [[body]], speech, and mind.
Having made these choices, they are reborn in a pleasing world,
where pleasing [[contact]]s strike them.
Touched by pleasing [[contact]]s, they experience pleasing feelings that are exclusively happy—like the [[gods]] replete with glory.
Furthermore, another person makes both hurtful and pleasing choices by way of [[body]], speech, and mind.
Having made these choices, they are reborn in a world that is both hurtful and pleasing,
where hurtful and pleasing [[contact]]s strike them.
Touched by both hurtful and pleasing [[contact]]s, they experience both hurtful and pleasing feelings that are a mixture of pleasure and pain—like humans, some [[gods]], and some beings in the underworld.
These are the three people found in the world.”
