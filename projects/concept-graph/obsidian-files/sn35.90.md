“Mendicants, turbulence is a disease, a boil, a dart.
That’s why the Realized One lives unperturbed, with dart drawn out.
Now, a [[mendicant]] might wish: ‘May I live unperturbed, with dart drawn out.’
So let them not [[conceive]]e anything to be the eye, let them not [[conceive]]e it in the eye, let them not [[conceive]]e it as the eye, let them not [[conceive]]e that ‘the eye is mine.’
Let them not [[conceive]]e sights …
eye [[consciousness]] …
eye [[contact]] …
Let them not [[conceive]]e anything to be the pleasant, painful, or neutral feeling that arises conditioned by eye [[contact]]. Let them not [[conceive]]e it in that, let them not [[conceive]]e it as that, and let them not [[conceive]]e that ‘that is mine.’
Let them not [[conceive]]e anything to be the ear …
nose …
tongue …
[[body]] …
mind …
thoughts …
mind [[consciousness]] …
mind [[contact]] …
Let them not [[conceive]]e anything to be the pleasant, painful, or neutral feeling that arises conditioned by mind [[contact]]. Let them not [[conceive]]e it in that, let them not [[conceive]]e it as that, and let them not [[conceive]]e that ‘that is mine.’
Let them not [[conceive]]e anything to be all, let them not [[conceive]]e it in all, let them not [[conceive]]e it as all, let them not [[conceive]]e that ‘all is mine’.
Not [[conceive]]ing, they don’t grasp at anything in the world.
Not grasping, they’re not anxious. Not being anxious, they personally become extinguished.
They understand: ‘Rebirth is ended, the spiritual journey has been completed, what had to be done has been done, there is no return to any state of existence.’”
